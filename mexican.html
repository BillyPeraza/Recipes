1 Horchata

Horchata

Beverage

Ingredients (1 Gallon)

  • 2 cups long-grain white rice (uncooked, not parboiled)
  • 4 medium (3") canela sticks (~1ft worth)
  • 8 cups hot tap water (for soaking)
  • 1 can evaporated milk (12 oz each) OR 4 cups whole milk
  • 2 cans (14 oz each) sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1½ teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon fine salt (optional)
  • - Cold water & ice to bring final volume up to 1 gallon
  • - Ground cinnamon (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. 1
    Prep
    Rinse rice until water runs mostly clear. Break cinnamon sticks into pieces. Combine rice + cinnamon + 8 cups hot tap water in large bowl. Cover and refrigerate 8–12 hours.
  2. 2
    Blend
    In batches, blend rice, cinnamon, and soaking water until smooth (~1 min per batch)
  3. 3
    Strain
    In a large container, strain each batch through fine mesh sieve lined with cheesecloth
  4. 4
    Blend
    Re-blend strained solids with the 2 cups of whole milk, strain again
  5. 4
    Mix
    Stir in cans of condensed & evaporated milk, vanilla, and ¼ tsp salt
  6. 5
    Chill
    Chill well (1–2 hours). Serve over ice with ground cinnamon
Soak Time: 8-12 hours
Prep Time: 1 hour
Chill Time: 1-2 hours
Yield: 1 gallon (16 servings)
