Spiced Chicken Thighs

Meal Prep

Spice Mix (5 Servings)

  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 4 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons pepper

Main Ingredients

  • 4 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 2 cups bone broth (for cooking rice)

Vegetables

  • 4 medium zucchini
  • 16 oz mushrooms
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 large broccoli head
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. 1
    Prep
    Preheat oven to 400°F. Mix chili powder, salt, and pepper in a small bowl. Pat chicken thighs dry and rub with olive oil, then coat generously with spice mix.
  2. 2
    Chicken
    Place chicken thighs on a wire rack over a baking sheet. Bake for 25-30 minutes until internal temperature reaches 165°F.
  3. 3
    Broiler
    Switch oven to broil on high. Broil chicken for 3-5 minutes until crispy and golden brown. Watch closely to prevent burning. Let rest 10 minutes.
  4. 4
    Rice
    While chicken rests, cook 1.5 cups uncooked rice in 2 cups bone broth. Bring to boil, cover, reduce heat to low, cook 15 minutes. Remove from heat, let stand 5 minutes.
  5. 5
    Veggies
    Dice zucchini, mushrooms, onion, and broccoli into bite-sized pieces. Sauté in a large pan with olive oil over medium-high heat until tender and slightly browned, 8-10 minutes.
  6. 6
    Dice
    Dice chicken thighs into bite-sized pieces.
  7. 7
    Portion
    Divide rice, vegetables, diced chicken, and shredded cheese equally among 5 meal prep containers. Store refrigerated for up to 4 days or freeze for up to 3 months.
Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 35 min
Yield: 5 servings
Storage: 4 days fridge / 3 months freezer

Chicken Pesto Bake

Meal Prep

Main Ingredients

  • 1.25 cups rice (any type)
  • 2.2 lbs chicken breast (diced)
  • 3.5 oz green pesto (120 cal per 1.7 oz)
  • 11.3 oz low fat greek yogurt
  • 5 oz chopped spinach
  • 1 large red onion (finely diced)
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 5.3 oz sundried tomatoes

Instructions

  1. 1
    Prep
    Preheat oven to 360°F (180°C). Dice chicken breast into bite-sized pieces and finely dice the red onion.
  2. 2
    Mix
    In a large bowl, combine diced chicken, pesto, greek yogurt, chopped spinach, diced onion, and sundried tomatoes. Mix well to coat everything evenly.
  3. 3
    Layer
    Spread rice in the bottom of a 3-liter baking dish. Pour chicken stock over the rice. Top with the chicken and vegetable mixture.
  4. 4
    Bake
    Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 75-90 minutes until chicken is cooked through and rice is tender.
  5. 5
    Rest
    Remove from oven and let rest for 10 minutes before serving. Divide into 4-6 portions for meal prep.

Nutrition (per serving - 6 servings)

Calories: 484
Carbs: 47.2g
Fat: 8.89g
Protein: 51.4g
Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 75-90 min
Yield: 4-6 servings
Storage: 4 days fridge

Egg Bake Combo (2 Flavors)

Meal Prep

Base Ingredients (5 Servings)

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup liquid egg whites (250ml)
  • 16 oz low-fat cottage cheese (454g)
  • 3 g salt (1/2 tsp)
  • 2 g black pepper (1 tsp)

Flavor Option A: Veggies & Pesto (3 containers)

  • 3 oz fresh spinach, chopped (85g)
  • 12 cherry tomatoes, halved (200g)
  • 3 tbsp pesto (45g)

Flavor Option B: Bacon & Cheese (2 containers)

  • 1 package uncured Canadian bacon (6-8 slices)
  • 2 bell peppers (mixed colors)
  • 1 small red onion
  • 1 8oz block sharp cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. 1
    Prep
    Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease 5 square glass containers (8x8 inch or similar).
  2. 2
    Base
    Blend eggs, egg whites, cottage cheese, salt, and pepper until smooth. Divide evenly among 5 containers.
  3. 3
    Veggies
    For 3 containers: Add spinach, cherry tomatoes, and 1 tbsp pesto to each. Mix gently.
  4. 4
    Bacon
    For 2 containers: Add diced bacon, bell peppers, red onion, and cheddar cheese to each. Mix gently.
  5. 5
    Bake
    Bake for 15-20 minutes until set and slightly golden. Let cool before covering.
  6. 6
    Store
    Refrigerate for up to 4 days. Reheat in microwave for 1-2 minutes before serving.

Nutrition (per serving)

Veggies & Pesto: ~26g protein
Bacon & Cheese: ~28g protein
Calories: 280-320
Carbs: 8-12g
Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 15-20 min
Yield: 5 servings
Storage: 4 days fridge
